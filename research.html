Research Interests

Geometry

Lie theory, smooth manifolds, Lie groups, Lie racks, and the coquecigrue problem.

Logic

Fuzzy set theory, fuzzy logic, and fuzzy abstract algebra (e.g., fuzzy vector spaces, fuzzy Lie algebras).

Knowledge Representation

Building ontologies for AI implementations (e.g., Natural Language Processing), and fuzzy reasoning in crisp ontologies (e.g., FuzzyOWL 2).
